About Our Factory

The factory is located in Beni-Sweif government about 120 Km from Cairo city, it has many facilities including:
  • A vegetables processing line (Peas, Carrots, Beans, Okra, Colcasia and Green Beans) With a Capacity of 3.8 tons/hour, Except for the peas as it reaches 5 ton/hour.
  • A vegetable leaves processing line (Spinach & Molokhia) 3 tons/hour.
  • Three automated packing lines with a capacity of 100/400gm bags/min.
  • A storage Capacity of 5000 metric tons
  • A building which contains all the equipment’s, utilities and the biggest lab in the food industry sector with a 280 square meters space.
  • Worker lockers that accommodates 300 workers and related administration fees.
